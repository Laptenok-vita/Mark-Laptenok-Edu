Personal learning experiences

Professional environment

For the purposes of my current job, I have learned iSpring Suite and the iSpring Online learning management system on my own. I was given a task to create a small course by the head of the teaching and development department. The task was needed to demonstrate my skills in instructional design and the ability to learn those are needed for the position I was applying for. I chose the learning strategy and the appropriate content, then developed a design for the course. At the final stage, I packaged the course using Microsoft PowerPoint and iSpring Suite. When I was not able to find intuitively the appropriate tool to fulfill a part of my design I used the official help articles provided by Microsoft and iSpring.
