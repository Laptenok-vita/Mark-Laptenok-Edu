Personal learning experiences

Elementary school

I was learning the Russian spelling rules. The teacher made the series of paper cards with words written on them and the several slots in the places where the letters should be determined by a pupil according with the rules. I received a card, put a sheet of paper under the card and filled the gaps in the words to make them spelled correctly. This exercise helped me learn the correct spelling of certain words; it is crucial for having a good command of language.
